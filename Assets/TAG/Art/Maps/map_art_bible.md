# TAG Map Art Bible

## Rooftop

Commercial target: a golden-hour city rooftop chase playground with rooftop gardens, water towers, AC units, billboards, bridge gaps, neon signs, construction props, fire escapes, a helipad, skyline cards, wind VFX, birds, distant traffic, and dynamic sunset lighting.

## Jungle

Commercial target: a living green arena with dense vegetation, oversized trees, vines, waterfalls, ruins, temples, hidden shortcuts, wildlife silhouettes, fog banks, leaf shafts, birds, insects, falling leaves, and dynamic tropical weather.

## Mine

Commercial target: an underground crystal chase space with mine carts, rails, crystal caverns, wooden supports, cave openings, lava sections, elevators, dynamite props, hidden tunnels, dust motes, echo reverb, flickering lanterns, and colored crystal lighting.
