# TAG Creature Style Guide

Every TAG creature should be collectible, readable, and animation-friendly.

- Unique silhouette before color is applied.
- Cute idle loop, sprint loop, dash burst, grapple reaction, emotes, and victory animation.
- Rarity tiers: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic.
- Unlockable skins must stay cosmetic only and never change competitive power.
- Avoid generic humans, robots, sci-fi soldiers, or space-themed silhouettes.
- Target inspirations: Pokémon memorability, Fall Guys charm, Stumble Guys accessibility, Clash Royale appeal, and Brawl Stars personality.
